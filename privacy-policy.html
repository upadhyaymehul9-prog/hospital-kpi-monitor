Privacy Policy

Hospital KPI Monitor  ·  Effective: March 20, 2026

Summary: Hospital KPI Monitor does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It works completely offline and requires no login.

1. Introduction

Hospital KPI Monitor ("the App") is a free Progressive Web App (PWA) developed by Mehul Upadhyay. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

2. No Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required
  • No names, emails, or contact details are collected
  • No location data is accessed
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes

3. Offline Functionality

The App is designed to work fully offline. All KPI calculations and WHO benchmark comparisons are performed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server.

4. Local Storage

The App may use your device's local storage solely to save your preferences and cached data for offline use. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

The App is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (such as IP addresses) as part of their hosting infrastructure. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the App is safe for use by all age groups.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
